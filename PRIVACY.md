# Privacy Policy — Rósa Parks

**Controller:** Sósíalistaflokkur Íslands (Id. No. 560914-0240), registered office Hverfisgötu 105, 101 Reykjavík.

**Contact:** `xj@xj.is`

## What we collect

When you start a parking session:

- Vehicle registration number (*bílnúmer*) — as you type it.
- Zone identifier (P1–P4) — as you select it.
- Start and end timestamps.
- Mobile telephone number — for payment and receipt handling (per the joint-controller agreement with the City of Reykjavík).

We do **not** collect: location history, device identifiers, contacts, analytics events, advertising identifiers, or any account information. No user accounts are required.

## Lawful basis

Processing is carried out under Article 6(1)(e) GDPR (task carried out in the public interest) and Article 26 GDPR (joint controllership), in accordance with Iceland's Data Protection Act No. 90/2018 (*lög nr. 90/2018 um persónuvernd og vinnslu persónuupplýsinga*).

## Joint controllership

Rósa Parks and the City of Reykjavík (*Reykjavíkurborg*, through Bílastæðasjóður) are joint controllers for the purpose of collecting parking fees. A written joint-controller agreement (*samkomulag um sameiginlega ábyrgð*) governs the division of responsibilities.

## Data location

Application traffic is routed through Cloudflare's Keflavík point-of-presence for Icelandic users. No personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area.

## Retention

- Active session data: retained by us until you end the session, or 24 hours maximum.
- Completed session data: transferred to the City's central parking system; retention is governed by the City's own policy and Icelandic archives law (Act 77/2014).

## Your rights

You may exercise your rights under the Data Protection Act — access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection — by contacting either controller:

- Sósíalistaflokkur Íslands: `xj@xj.is`
- Bílastæðasjóður Reykjavíkur: via <https://reykjavik.is>

You may also lodge a complaint with the Icelandic Data Protection Authority (*Persónuvernd*): <https://personuvernd.is>.
